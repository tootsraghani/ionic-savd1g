About the Application

The calorie intake calculator app helps you determine how many calories you should eat in 1 day. It can also help you determine where you can start from when you either want to gain weight or to lose weight.

How does the daily calorie intake app actually work? First, our app determines your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) through a formula which is different for men and women. After calculating your BMR, it is multiplied by a factor specific to how much physical activity you have in a week:

Lifesyle Amount of Physical Activity Factor
Sedentary lifestyle Little or no exercise 1.2
Slightly active lifestyle Light exercise or sports 1-2 days/week 1.4
Moderately active lifestyle Moderate exercise or sports 2-3 days/week 1.6
Very active lifestyle Hard exercise or sports 4-5 days/week 1.75
Extra active lifestyle Very hard exercise or sports 6-7 days/week 2.0
Professional athlete More than Extra Active 2.3

Source: https://www.omnicalculator.com/health/calorie
